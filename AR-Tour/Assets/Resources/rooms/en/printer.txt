Printer instructions:

1. Touch the screen
2. Hold the card in front of the 
   card reader for approx. 3 seconds
3. Enter your PIN
4. Click on "Check balance"
5. Confirm the following message 
   with "Yes"
6. Now wait until the banknote slot 
   lights up red and insert your 
   banknote. The booking process 
   takes a good half a minute
7. Click on "Exit no receipt".
