UX/Al Lab
In the User Experience Lab, new learning 
scenarios in the context of Industry 4.0 
are investigated as well as research 
projects in areas such as digital learning 
and automotive software are carried out. 
The best-known application of eye tracking 
software comes from the field of marketing. 
Another application is the investigation of 
user-friendly, experience-oriented design of 
user interfaces in the context of 
human-machine interaction.
