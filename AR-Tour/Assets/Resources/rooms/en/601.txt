A FabLab is an open workshop and
offers production equipment and modern
industrial production processes for the
production of individual pieces. At
FabLab of the DHBW Heidenheim, techniques such as 3D printing, laser cutters,
CNC machines and an electronics
workshop are made available to students and others.
Link:https://www.heidenheim.dhbw.de/forschung-transfer/labore/fablab
