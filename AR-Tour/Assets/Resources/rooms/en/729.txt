Student Counseling
The student counseling service helps 
anyone who is interested in a dual 
study program at the DHBW Heidenheim 
or needs information about a specific 
course. It also advices students in 
choosing the right course of study. 
The counseling can be by telephone, 
e-mail or in person (if possible 
with an appointment).
