The headmaster of the DHBW Heidenheim
The presidency of the DHBW is appointed 
at each DHBW location by the headmaster 
of the study academy. Since 2017, Prof. 
Dr.-Ing. Dr. Rainer Przywara has been 
the headmaster of DHBW Heidenheim. The 
father of three studied mechanical 
engineering at the University of Hanover 
and earned his doctorate in manufacturing 
engineering at the University of Hanover. 
He is also a trained historian. At the 
University of Gloucester in Great Britain, 
he earned the the title of Doctor of 
Philosophy. In addition to his scientific 
career, the professor has also worked 
as a managing director in the automotive 
industry for development and as a senior 
employee.
